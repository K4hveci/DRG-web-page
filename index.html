We are a group of 3 cousins who have come together to form a research group, combining our diverse knowledge and experiences across various disciplines to create innovative solutions. Our goal is to contribute to the advancement of society through our scientific research and projects, while continually learning and exploring new ideas. By integrating our expertise from different fields, we focus on generating original ideas and achieving impactful results. We advance our research with collaboration and passion, aiming to solve tomorrow's challenges today.

Research interests

With a focus on "Optics for AI and AI for Optics", our current research interests are
- Optical computing
- Photonic neural networks
- Fiber optics and lasers
- Ultrafast optics and imaging
- Frequency conversion

Open Positions

In all of our activities, experiments, simulations and theoretical studies play an important role, and both experimentalists and theorists are welcome.
